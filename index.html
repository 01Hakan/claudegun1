About Lerna

Welcome to a world where creativity meets movement. I'm Lerna Babikyan, a passionate dance educator dedicated to nurturing young dancers and helping them discover their unique artistic voice.

With years of experience in creative dance education, I believe every child has the ability to express themselves through dance. My classes focus on building confidence, creativity, and coordination in a supportive and fun environment.

My teaching philosophy centers on the joy of movement, encouraging students to explore, imagine, and create while developing fundamental dance skills.

Our Classes

Creative dance programs tailored for every age group

Little Movers

Ages 4-7

Introduction to creative movement through play, stories, and imagination. Building coordination, rhythm, and self-expression in a nurturing environment.

Schedule: Tuesdays & Thursdays, 3:00 PM

Young Artists

Ages 8-12

Developing technique and artistry through creative choreography. Exploring various dance styles while encouraging individual expression and teamwork.

Schedule: Mondays & Wednesdays, 4:30 PM

Teen Expressions

Ages 13-17

Advanced creative dance for teens ready to push boundaries. Focus on contemporary techniques, improvisation, and personal artistic development.

Schedule: Fridays, 5:00 PM
